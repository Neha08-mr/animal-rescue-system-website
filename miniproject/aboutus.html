Our Mission

At Animal Rescue System, our mission is to rescue and rehabilitate injured and abandoned animals. We strive to provide a safe and nurturing environment for these animals while we work to find them loving forever homes. Our dedicated team of volunteers and staff are passionate about animal welfare and are committed to making a positive impact in our community.
